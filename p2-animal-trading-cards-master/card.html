Black mamba

The poison of Black mamba automatically enters the nerves, causing severe damage to the central nervous system and causing major organs to deteriorate.

  • Scientific Name: Dendroaspis polylepis
  • Average Length: 3.5 meters
  • Average Speed: 11 km/hr
  • Behavior: Graceful and unpredictable

It is agile and can move quickly. It is shy and secretive by nature and, like most snakes, avoids threats. In the wild, a black mamba seldom tolerates humans approaching more closely than about 40 meters.
